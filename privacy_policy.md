# Privacy Policy for BookPilot

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

## Overview

BookPilot ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our book summary and AI chat application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

### Usage Data
- App usage patterns and interactions within the application
- Book preferences and reading history
- Chat session data (stored locally on your device)
- Notes and summaries you create

### Device Information
- Device type, operating system, and basic hardware specifications
- Application crash reports and performance data
- IP address (for analytics purposes only)

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

#### Google Gemini API
- Through our Supabase edge functions, we send chat messages to Google's Gemini API for AI-powered responses
- Messages are processed temporarily and not stored permanently by us
- No personal identifiers are sent with chat requests

#### Supabase
- Used for hosting our edge functions that connect to Gemini API
- May log minimal technical data for operational purposes
- No personal data is stored on Supabase servers

#### GitHub
- Books data is hosted on public GitHub repositories
- Access to book content is anonymous and does not require personal information

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **App Functionality**: To provide book summaries, AI chat features, and note-taking capabilities
2. **Performance Improvement**: To identify and fix bugs, optimize app performance
3. **User Experience**: To understand how users interact with the app and improve features
4. **Local Storage**: To cache book data and save your notes/summaries on your device

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Data
- All your notes, summaries, and chat history are stored locally on your device
- We do not have access to your personal notes or chat conversations
- Data remains on your device unless you choose to export it

### Cloud Data
- Only book content (summary, metadata) is fetched from our public GitHub repository
- No personal user data is stored in the cloud
- AI processing happens through secure API calls without storing personal data

## Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. The only data shared is:

1. **AI Processing**: Chat messages sent to Google Gemini API through Supabase edge functions for AI responses
2. **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics that do not contain personal information
3. **Public Content**: Book data fetched from public GitHub repositories

## Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

1. **Access**: View the data stored on your device through the app
2. **Modify**: Edit or delete your notes and summaries
3. **Export**: Export your saved content in various formats
4. **Delete**: Clear all local data through the app's settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages but parental guidance is recommended for younger users engaging with AI chat features.

## Third-Party Links and Services

Our app may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. Please review their privacy policies when using external links.

## Data Retention

- Local data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
- Temporary session data is cleared when the app is closed
- No personal data is retained on our servers

## Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- Local data encryption where supported by the device
- Secure API connections using HTTPS/TLS
- Minimal data collection and processing
- Regular security updates and monitoring

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
